Inspired by modern magazine layouts with vibrant orange/red accents on cream/white backgrounds.

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Vibrant Orange/Red**: `#ff3d00` (vibrant-500)
  - Use for: Headlines, CTAs, borders, accents
  
- **Bright Orange**: `#f97316` (primary-500)
  - Use for: Secondary accents, gradients

### Backgrounds
- **Cream**: `#fefdfb` (cream-50) - Main background
- **White**: `#ffffff` - Sections, cards
- **Light Cream**: `#faf8f3` (cream-200) - Subtle backgrounds

### Text
- **Dark Gray**: `#111827` (gray-900) - Headlines
- **Medium Gray**: `#374151` (gray-700) - Body text  
- **Light Gray**: `#6b7280` (gray-500) - Secondary text

## Typography
- **Headings**: font-extrabold, tracking-tight
- **Body**: font-medium
- **Buttons**: font-bold

## Design Elements
- Bold borders (`border-4`, `border-l-4`)
- Vibrant accent lines (`h-1.5 bg-vibrant-500`)
- Subtle shadows
- Clean, minimalist cards
- High contrast
